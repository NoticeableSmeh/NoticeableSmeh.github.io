CPU Rasterizer | From Pixels to Triangles

Work in progress. Building a software rasterizer in C++ on the CPU using SDL2 and stb_image. Part 1 sets up the framebuffer and image dumps, Part 2 draws lines and trims branches toward a Bresenham variant, Part 3 adds a clear geometry header with vec2 and vec3 operations for wireframe and triangle fill.
