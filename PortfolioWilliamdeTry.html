Medieval Night Scene in Unreal Engine 5 Link

I wanted to explore the process of building a small, atmospheric environment from scratch.
I modeled the houses in Maya, set up lighting and post-processing in Unreal Engine 5, and built custom fire and smoke particle systems in Niagara.
This scene helped me understand how lighting, materials, and particle systems work together to create believable real-time worlds.
